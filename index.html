Jonathan Zylberberg

Software Engineer

As a Full Stack Web Developer and graduate of Flatiron School, I code in languages like Ruby, Javascript ES6, HTML, CSS, and SQL, and am always interested in adding to this stack. I also work with frameworks and libraries like Rails, React, Redux, and Sinatra. Prior to Flatiron, I attended NYU, and graduated with a B.A. in Economics.

1

What led you to what you are doing now?

Growing up I formed a love for technology by playing video games, like Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles, after school on my parents Atari ST. When my family acquired our first desktop computer, I was fascinated by it and would spend as much time as I could exploring the web. This remained a passion throughout my time at NYU, where I majored in Economics. I felt the pull to continue to learn more about technology, and after graduation decided to dedicate myself to becoming the best web developer I could be. I love writing code because I enjoy the ability to create amazing things. Nothing makes me happier than seeing people use the applications I create.

What do you think healthcare will look like in 2020?

I believe that healthcare is moving toward alternative payment models and toward payment methods that drive quality and reduce costs. I also believe that IT will play a major role in the success of these programs, and will help patients and providers be able to better navigate the complex healthcare system in a simpler and safer manner.

What is the accomplishment you are most proud of?

An accomplishment that really stand out for me was making my school's basketball team when I was younger. When I originally tried out for the team I didn’t make it. I was heartbroken, but I saw this as an opportunity to better myself. I trained everyday, and did everything I could in order to become better and better. The next year, I made the team and by the following year I was Captain and MVP. From this opportunity I saw how important it is to work hard for your dream, and that determination, grit, dedication and a strong mentality can lead to both growth and to success.

I am extremely grateful for these opportunities I have had, I want to try my best to continue traveling in my free time so I can over time, see the world.
